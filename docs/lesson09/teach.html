Assignment

Learn more about the pandas module by writing more Python code that manipulates the water meter readings from the City of Rexburg. Write a Python program that uses the pandas module to answer these questions:

Core Requirements

  1. select and unique
    1. For the data stored in water.csv, how many unique account types are there? What are those unique account types?
    2. How many meters are listed in the file (not how many meter readings, how many unique meterNumbers)?
  2. filter and sum
    1. What is the total amount of water used?
    2. What is the total amount of water used by residences?
    3. What is the total amount of water used by apartments?
  3. drop_duplicates and value_counts
    1. How many meters of each account type are in the file?

Stretch Challenges

  1. filter between two dates
    1. What is the amount of water used in 2018?
  2. drop_duplicates and sum
    1. What is the number of dwellings in Rexburg?
  3. filter and sum
    1. What is the average amount of water used by each dwelling?
