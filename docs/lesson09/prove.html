Assignment

During this lesson and the next lesson, you will write a Python program that uses pandas to analyze water usage data from the City of Rexburg to answer these two questions:

  • Is the total amount of water used in the City of Rexburg increasing each year?
  • Is the average amount of water used per dwelling increasing each year?

If you examine closely the water usage data, you will see that some water meters serve both a business and one or more apartments. When analyzing data to answer the second question, your program should exclude the water usage data for such meters because there is no way to determine how much water went to the business and how much went to the attached apartment. In fact, when answering the second question, your program should use only the data for apartment complexes, residences, town homes, and trailer courts.
